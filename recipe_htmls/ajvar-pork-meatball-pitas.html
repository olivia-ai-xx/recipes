Ajvar Pork Meatball Pitas

Servings
2
Time
30 mins
Main Ingredients
Pork Mince, Ajvar, Pita
Calories
~700 kcal / serve
🥬 Ingredients
Pork Meatballs
  • 300g lean pork mince
  • 1 egg
  • 30g panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tbsp fresh flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
Creamy Ajvar Sauce
  • 120g I Love Ajvar paprika relish
  • 80g Woolworths light Greek yogurt
  • 80ml chicken stock
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
Cabbage Slaw
  • 150g green cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 60g carrot, grated
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp olive oil
To Serve
  • 2 Zeus Street Greek pita breads (Woolworths)
  • Fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • Lemon wedges
🍳 Recipe
  1. Combine the pork mince, egg, panko breadcrumbs, garlic, oregano, parsley, smoked paprika, and a generous pinch of salt and pepper in a bowl. Mix until just combined — don't overwork. Shape into 12 even meatballs (about 40g each).
  2. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Cook the meatballs for 10–12 minutes, turning every 2–3 minutes, until deep golden all over and cooked through with no pink inside.
  3. While the meatballs cook, make the creamy ajvar sauce: stir together the ajvar, light Greek yogurt, chicken stock, garlic, and lemon juice until smooth. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss the cabbage and carrot together. Drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and toss well to coat. Set aside — the slaw will soften slightly as it sits, which is ideal.
  5. Warm the pita breads in a dry pan for 1–2 minutes per side over medium heat until pliable and lightly charred, or wrap in foil and heat in the oven at 180°C for 5 minutes.
  6. To assemble: spread a generous spoonful of creamy ajvar sauce over each pita. Pile on the slaw, then the meatballs (6 per serve). Spoon over a little extra sauce, scatter with fresh parsley, and serve with lemon wedges.

Notes

  • Meatballs can be shaped a day ahead and refrigerated raw — cook straight from the fridge, adding 2 extra minutes to the cook time.
  • The creamy ajvar sauce doubles as a great dip or spread — make a double batch and keep refrigerated for up to 3 days.
  • Zeus Street Greek pita breads are stocked at Woolworths and are thicker and fluffier than standard flatbreads, making them ideal for loading up.
